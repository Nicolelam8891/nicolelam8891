 ##  ğŸ‹ More info about me ğŸ‹ 

As a first-generation Asian American, I understand the struggles of growing up in an underserved community, not understanding English and being different. This experience fueled my passion for helping others, leading me to an 11-month accelerated Bachelor of Science in Nursing at Regis University. My healthcare journey began as an inpatient and outpatient nurse. During these 5 years of being a nurse, I had a close ally: the EPIC charting system. Working with EPIC was a revelation. It not only streamlined my nursing tasks but also played a pivotal role in reducing medical errors and preventing adverse effects. Whether at the bedside or in the clinic, I saw firsthand how technology made my life easier as a nurse, as long as I spent the time to learn more about the system--which is exactly what I did! I also worked closely with MyChart, another EPIC resource, to demonstrate how technology could empower patients, making health management accessible! 

This exposure sparked a profound appreciation for technology and its potential to transform lives, which is why I decided to pivot my career, steering towards the tech world with a mission to make a difference through coding! Without any experience in tech or coding, I joined the Turing School of Software & Design. It's a 7-month intensive program, and I've just wrapped up Mod 3. Next up, Mod 4!
